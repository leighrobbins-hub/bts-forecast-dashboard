Checkpoint quiz

Four questions (+25 XP each)

1. Constant of proportionality k in y = kx means…

k is the factor relating y to x.

2. A line through (0,0) and (2,6) has slope…

Rise/run = 6/2 = 3.

3. Which point must a proportional graph through the origin pass?

Zero input → zero output in proportional stories from zero.

4. If 8 ounces cost $2, the unit price is…

2 dollars ÷ 8 ounces = $0.25 per ounce.
